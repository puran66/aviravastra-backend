## 🎨 DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Color Palette
- **Kanjeevaram**: Deep maroon (#800020) with rich gold (#FFD700)
- **Paithani**: Royal purple (#6A0DAD) with bright gold (#FFC107)
- **Gadwal**: Auspicious red (#DC143C) with traditional gold
- **Banarasi**: Emerald green (#50C878) with metallic gold
- **Pochampally**: Mustard yellow (#FFDB58) with maroon (#800000)

### Photography Style
- Professional e-commerce product photography
- Soft cream/beige backgrounds for products
- Studio lighting with soft shadows
- 8K quality, sharp focus
- Premium luxury aesthetic
- Traditional yet modern presentation

### Technical Specifications
- **Product Images**: Square format, 1000x1000px minimum
- **Banner Images**: Wide format, 1200x600px
- **File Format**: PNG for transparency support
- **Quality**: Maximum quality, optimized for web
